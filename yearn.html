Yearn

I yearn for a time that I had once enjoyed

The Internet and all its communities

Which has become

A Community.


One by one they arrived

Offering friendship

Offering connectivity untold

Innocent smiles

With sharpened FAANGs

They became irreplacable

And boiled us like the frogs we are.


Hobbies discarded

Friends lost

Homes evicted and demolished

We became Centralized.


In the abscence of havens

They cater to the Average Man

Minimalist rounded corners

Flat colors set the stage for the

Sans-serif hellscape

That Everyone can call home.


But proximity breeds resent

We All became One

In a way we were not programmed to do.


Watch the show;

A frantic race to the bottom

But you observe from within

We are the content

We are all along for the ride.


Something was lost

In this brief history

A DEAD INTERNET left in its wake.
